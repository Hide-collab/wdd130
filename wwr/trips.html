Our Rafting Adventures

Choose from our exciting range of rafting trips designed for every skill level and adventure preference.

Los Dientes del Diablo

Difficulty: Advanced

Prepare for the ultimate adrenaline rush! Los Dientes del Diablo (The Devil's Teeth) features class IV-V rapids that will test even the most experienced rafters. Navigate through narrow gorges, powerful hydraulics, and massive standing waves. This full-day expedition includes a riverside lunch and is only recommended for physically fit adventurers with previous rafting experience.

  • Duration: Full Day (8 hours)
  • Class IV-V Rapids
  • Minimum Age: 16 years
  • Includes: Lunch, photos, all equipment

Big Mallard

Difficulty: Intermediate

The Big Mallard run offers the perfect balance of thrilling rapids and scenic beauty. Featuring consistent class III rapids with a few class II sections for recovery, this trip is ideal for those ready to step up from beginner runs. Paddle through stunning canyon walls, spot wildlife, and enjoy several exciting drops. This half-day adventure is our most popular trip!

  • Duration: Half Day (4 hours)
  • Class II-III Rapids
  • Minimum Age: 12 years
  • Includes: Snacks, photos, all equipment

Family Float Adventure

Difficulty: Beginner

Perfect for families and first-time rafters! Our Family Float Adventure provides a gentle introduction to river rafting with easy class I-II rapids. Float through peaceful stretches, enjoy stunning scenery, and learn basic paddling techniques from our friendly guides. This relaxing half-day trip includes time for swimming and is suitable for all ages. Create lasting memories with your loved ones on this scenic journey!

  • Duration: Half Day (3 hours)
  • Class I-II Rapids
  • Minimum Age: 5 years
  • Includes: Snacks, all equipment
